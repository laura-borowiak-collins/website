About

Counsellor, writer, and installation artist

Laura Collins holds a Master of Counselling from Monash University and a Master of Writing for Performance from the University of Melbourne. Passionate about supporting vulnerable populations, she specializes in integrating creative and compassionate approaches to mental health care.

As a writer and installation artist, her work explores themes of disability, neurodiversity, and the climate crisis, merging creativity with pressing social issues.
